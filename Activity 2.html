Student Bio

Catherine

Catherine earned her BA in Music and Shakespeare Studies from William and Mary in 2018 and her MSc in Information and Library Studies from the University of Strathclyde in 2020. She teaches group fitness classes and enjoys singing, baking, and reading. She studied voice with Mary Fletcher for six semesters at W&M while pursuing various performance outlets on campus, including the Botetourt Chamber Singers and the Cleftomaniacs. While there, she could also be found working as a student assistant in the Music Library. Catherine has soloed with the Williamsburg Choral Guild, Hickory Neck Episcopal Church, and the First Church Of Christ, Scientist.
